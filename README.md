# Resourceman Mode

A RuneLite plugin that enforces self-obtained resources only. All resources must be gathered yourself through skilling, monster drops, quest rewards, or group content — you cannot buy, trade, or pick up resources from other players.

## Rules

- All resources must be **self-obtained**
- You **can** buy weapons and armour on the Grand Exchange
- You **can** trade weapons and armour with other players
- You **cannot** buy resources on the Grand Exchange
- You **cannot** receive resources through player trades
- You **cannot** pick up resources dropped by other players
- NPC shops can be toggled on or off in settings

## Features

### Enforcement
- **Grand Exchange** — resource items are greyed out in search and cannot be selected
- **Player Trades** — Accept button is blocked if the other player's offer contains resources
- **Ground Items** — Take option is silently removed for resources dropped by other players
- **NPC Shops** — Buying from shops can be toggled on or off

### Resource Tracking
- Tracks all resources gathered through skilling, monster drops, and raids
- Displays all-time and session totals in the side panel
- Shows item icons next to each tracked resource
- Persistent tracking across sessions

### Customisation
- Choose your sidebar icon from a selection of OSRS items
- Toggle NPC shop enforcement on or off
- Toggle the violation sound on or off
- Toggle the violation chat message on or off
- Reset all tracking data from the panel (requires double confirmation)
